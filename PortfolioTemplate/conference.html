About Me

Currently, I am in my junior year at Iowa State University, where I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. I have worked on a variety of projects in the past. I have used Java, C, C#, Javascript, Typescript in the past, and also have experience with HTML, CSS, jQuery, Angular2+, SQL, Android Studio, Git, and more.

Some of my personal interests include playing the Trumpet, Bass Guitar, and Keys, fiddling around with Synthesizers, music production, playing sports such as Basketball, Tennis, and Ping-pong, staying late trying to complete that board game that takes way to long, and more.

I am always willing to discuss internship positions, full-time offers, or projects. I can be contacted via phone, email, or through LinkedIn.
